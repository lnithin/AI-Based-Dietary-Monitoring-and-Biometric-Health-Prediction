## â“ FAQs

### Q: Why is my BMI marked as "Estimated"?
**A:** Your account existed before the BMI system was added. Default values are being used. Update your height, weight, and age in Profile to get accurate BMI.

### Q: How often should I update my weight?
**A:** Update whenever your weight changes significantly (Â±1kg). The system will recalculate BMI automatically.

### Q: Can I change my height?
**A:** Yes, you can update height anytime in Profile settings. BMI will recalculate.

### Q: Is my data private?
**A:** Yes! All calculations are done securely on the server. Your biometric data is never shared.

### Q: What if I don't want to provide my weight?
**A:** The system will continue working with estimated values, but recommendations will be less accurate. We encourage updating for better health insights.

### Q: Does BMI work for children?
**A:** Yes! For users under 20, the system uses pediatric BMI classifications based on age and sex.

## ðŸ“ž Need Help?

### Common Issues

**Issue:** "Complete Profile" prompt won't go away
- **Solution:** Make sure you entered ALL three: age, height, weight
- Click "Save Changes"
- Refresh the page

**Issue:** BMI seems incorrect
- **Solution:** Double-check height (should be in cm, not inches)
- Verify weight (should be in kg, not lbs)
- Recalculate: 1 inch = 2.54 cm, 1 lb = 0.45 kg

**Issue:** Can't see BMI card
- **Solution:** Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try logging out and back in
